This directory contains modeling references for the six representative hero
species. Each species has four core complementary views, with a few extra
stem, bud, and plant-context shots where they help expose attachment geometry
and growth habit.

## Intended coverage

- **Front/center:** petal count, overlap, curvature, reproductive structures,
  color variation, and vein direction.
- **Side/calyx/bracts/labellum:** petal depth, attachment, underside anatomy,
  species-specific supporting structures, and silhouette.
- **Habit/foliage/fruit/stem:** leaves, stems, buds or seed structures, scale,
  posture, and the relationship between the flower and the rest of the plant,
  especially at the flower-to-stem junction.

The downloaded images are approximately 1600--1920 pixels on their longest
requested dimension. They are intended as design and validation references;
consult the linked source page before redistributing or publishing an image.
